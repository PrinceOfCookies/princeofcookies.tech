Data Loss Averted - How to Backup Your Dupes!

Losing important data is always a pain. I did a factory reset not long ago and lost a lot of important data because I was limited in what I could transfer over. That's why backups are important. Luckily, Low Sodium has a custom feature built into Advanced Duplicator 2 that lets you backup your dupes to the server, which means even if you wipe your Garry's Mod installation clean, your dupes are still safe in the server.

Backing up your dupes

Backing up your dupes is useful in case of extreme data loss or if you reset your Garry's Mod installation. Remember, your dupes are in the data folder. Backing up works by uploading your dupe files to the server, where it gives your dupe a unique identifier along with a whitelist, which means if someone has the unique identifier, they still require permission to use it. No one has access to your dupes unless they're the server operator.

Step 1: Choose the dupe you want to backup and then right click on the dupe and click on "Backup to Server".

Your dupe can now be found under the folder "Advanced Duplicator Backups". If you ever lose your dupes, you can right click on "Advanced Duplicator Backups" and press "Download All Backups" and they'll appear in the folder again.

You can also back them up yourself (i.e., put them on the cloud). Navigate to "steam\steamapps\common\GarrysMod\garrysmod\data\advdupe2" and copy your dupes to a different folder. You can backup that folder to external cloud servers like mega.nz or drive.google.com.
