Next-Day Delivery: How to Play Mailman

Mailman is a custom job on Low Sodium DarkRP. The task is simple but confusing to new players. This guide explains how to play effectively, the job’s skill levels, and unique quirks.

Picking up and Delivering

The job is easy. Even a 5-year-old could do it. This is broken into two parts.

Picking up a Package

You spawn with a random mailbox assigned (6 total):

  • Downtown, Bank
  • Industrial, Building 4
  • Joint, Intersection
  • Slums, Strip Club
  • Downtown, Cafe
  • Residential, Garage
  1. Look for the "Collect Mail" icon
  2. Go to the assigned mailbox
  3. Press your +use key (usually E)

Delivering the Package

The package will show who it’s for, size (small/medium/big), and origin.

No tracker is available until Mailman Level 15. Use PMs to locate players or arrange a meetup.

  1. Find the player
  2. Yell until they open it

If they’re AFK and you have Mailman Level 10, use "HERE’S YOUR PACKAGE" to force open the box.

Skill Levels

  • Level 10: HERE’S YOUR PACKAGE – force open package
  • Level 15: Mailman Tracker I – shows distance on box
  • Level 25: Playermodel: Boxman

General Info

Package contents may include:

  • Weapons
  • Potions
  • Meth (Bags or Crates)
  • Health Vials
  • Suit Batteries
Warning: Mailboxes can be hacked. You might deliver a mailbomb. Back away fast after delivery if suspicious.
